SillcoxWeb Privacy Statement

Effective Date: April 30, 2019

Thanks for entrusting SillcoxWeb for your academic needs. It's through the collective community
and participants such as yourself that help the site. Holding onto private information is a serious
responsibility and we want you to know how were handling it.



Section Information
Login Credentials No passwords are read from text or stored as plain text. All passwords are encrypted and saved into a table database (SQL). Username and emails
are stored in the database under strict security. Emails are also saved on the local server for testing and backup purposes.
Cookies No cookies are saved at all or implemented.
Location Your location is never tracked or saved.
Contacting Us Please feel free to contact us about comments, questions, or concerns through the feedback message input inside the site. Or contact us through @sillcoxhelp@gmail.com
